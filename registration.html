Join the Challenge

Register your team and be a part of the future of engineering education.

The *EduAIthon 2026* is a premier 48-hour challenge designed to spur innovation in engineering education using *Artificial Intelligence*. We welcome diverse, interdisciplinary teams eager to solve real-world problems in this domain. By registering, your team secures a spot in this highly competitive event, providing networking opportunities, expert mentorship, and a platform to launch your project globally. Ensure your team meets all guidelines and submits the required documents before the final deadline.

Participation Guidelines

  • Open to all *undergraduate and postgraduate* students.
  • *Team Formation:* Teams must consist of *3–4 students*, preferably from different disciplines (interdisciplinary teams encouraged).
  • Each team should be guided by one *faculty mentor* (can be in-person or online).
  • Registration must be completed through the official EduAIthon portal.

Required Submissions

These documents are required during the online registration process. Ensure clarity and conciseness in your proposal.

  • *Problem Statement:* A concise description of the challenge your team will address (max *150 words*).
  • *Initial Idea Outline:* A summary of your proposed solution and approach (max *300 words*).

Why You Should Register Your Team

  • *Guaranteed Slot:* Secure your team's place in the competitive 48-hour challenge.
  • *Expert Mentorship:* Get exclusive access to industry leaders and faculty for guidance during the event.
  • *Resource Access:* Unlock all necessary APIs, datasets, and technical documentation needed for the project.
  • *Certification & Swag:* Receive official participation certificates and event merchandise/swag.
  • *Pitch Platform:* Gain the opportunity to present your innovative project to a panel of investors and experts.

Final Submission Deadline

15 Nov 2025

Don't miss the chance to innovate. All required documents must be submitted online by this date.

Core Theme & Judging Criteria

The challenge is structured around a central theme and well-defined judging criteria to ensure excellence and fairness.

Core Theme

  • Empowering Self-Learning through AI: Building Personalized Learning Tools for Higher Education” This unique hackathon invites faculty–student teams from higher-education institutions across India to design and prototype AI-enabled self-learning modules that help students learn specific chapters of a course independently. Teams will leverage freely available AI tools to create innovative, adaptive, and engaging learning experiences.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & Creativity: Originality and uniqueness of the solution.
  • Sustainability Impact: Contribution to eco-friendly, lasting solutions.
  • AI Integration Depth: Effective use of advanced technologies.
  • Heutagogical Relevance: Support for self-directed learning.
  • Presentation Clarity: Quality of communication.
